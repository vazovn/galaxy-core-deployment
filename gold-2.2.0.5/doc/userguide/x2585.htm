Deleting Jobs

To delete a job, use the command grmjob:

grmjob [—debug] [-? | —help] [—man] [—quiet] [-v | —verbose] [-V | —version] [[-j] gold_job_id | -J job_id]

Example 13-4. Deleting a job

$ grmjob -J PBS.1234.0 

Successfully deleted 1 Job
