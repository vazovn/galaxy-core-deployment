Deleting Accounts

To delete an account, use the command grmaccount:

grmaccount [—debug] [-? | —help] [—man] [—quiet] [-v | —verbose] {[-a] account_id}

Example 9-20. Deleting an account

$ grmaccount 2 

Successfully deleted 1 Account
